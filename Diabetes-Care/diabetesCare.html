Diabetes Care Guidelines

Stay strong, stay balanced — Manage your diabetes effectively.

🩺 Understanding Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar (glucose). Managing diabetes involves a balance of medication, healthy eating, regular physical activity, and routine monitoring.

💊 Commonly Prescribed Medicines

  • Metformin: Helps control blood sugar levels
  • Insulin (various types): For type 1 and severe type 2 diabetes
  • Glipizide: Stimulates insulin release
  • Sitagliptin: Enhances the body’s ability to control blood sugar
  • Empagliflozin: Helps kidneys remove sugar through urine

Consult your doctor before starting any medication.

🥗 Diet Tips for Diabetics

  • 🍚 Eat whole grains like brown rice, oats, quinoa
  • 🍎 Include high-fiber fruits like apples, berries
  • 🥦 Add non-starchy vegetables (broccoli, spinach)
  • 🧂 Avoid sugary drinks, fried food, excessive salt
  • 🧃 Drink plenty of water and avoid sweetened juices

🏃 Exercise & Lifestyle

  • 🚶 30-minute walk daily improves insulin sensitivity
  • 🏋️ Do light resistance training 2–3 times/week
  • 🧘 Manage stress with yoga and meditation
  • 🛏️ Maintain regular sleep schedule
  • 🚭 Quit smoking and limit alcohol
