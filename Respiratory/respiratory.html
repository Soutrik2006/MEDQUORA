Respiratory Care Guidelines

Every breath matters — Breathe better, live better.

🌬️ What is Respiratory Care?

Respiratory care focuses on supporting lung health and managing diseases like asthma, COPD, bronchitis, and pneumonia. It involves medication, breathing exercises, lifestyle changes, and avoiding pollutants and allergens.

💊 Commonly Prescribed Medicines

  • Salbutamol (Albuterol): Quick relief for asthma attacks
  • Budesonide: Reduces airway inflammation
  • Montelukast: Prevents wheezing and shortness of breath
  • Theophylline: Relaxes airway muscles to ease breathing
  • Tiotropium: Long-term bronchodilator for COPD

Use inhalers correctly and under medical supervision.

🍃 Lung Health Tips

  • 🚭 Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke exposure
  • 🌬️ Use air purifiers to reduce indoor pollutants
  • 🧼 Wash hands to prevent respiratory infections
  • 🏃‍♂️ Practice deep breathing or respiratory exercises
  • 💧 Stay hydrated to keep airways clear
