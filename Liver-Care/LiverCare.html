Liver Care Guidelines

Support your liver — Strengthen your body's natural detox system.

🩺 Understanding Liver Health

The liver is a vital organ responsible for filtering toxins, aiding digestion, and storing nutrients. Proper liver care helps prevent conditions like fatty liver, hepatitis, and cirrhosis.

🍏 Foods That Support Liver

  • Garlic: Activates liver enzymes to flush toxins
  • Leafy Greens: Neutralize heavy metals and pesticides
  • Green Tea: Contains antioxidants that protect the liver
  • Beetroot: Supports liver detox function
  • Turmeric: Reduces liver inflammation and aids bile production

Include these in moderation for optimal liver function.

🧴 Harmful Habits to Avoid

  • 🍻 Excessive alcohol consumption
  • 🍟 Fatty, fried, or processed foods
  • 💊 Overuse of medications or self-medication
  • 🛡️ Exposure to environmental toxins or unsafe supplements

🏃 Healthy Liver Lifestyle Tips

  • 🥗 Maintain a balanced, plant-rich diet
  • 🏋️ Exercise regularly to reduce fat buildup
  • 💧 Stay hydrated to help detox the liver
  • 🩺 Get vaccinated for hepatitis A & B
  • 🛏️ Sleep well and manage stress
