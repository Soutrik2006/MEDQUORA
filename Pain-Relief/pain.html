Pain Relief Guidelines

Ease the ache — Manage pain effectively and safely.

🧠 What is Pain Management?

Pain management includes strategies and treatments that reduce or control pain, improving quality of life. Pain can be acute (short-term) or chronic (long-lasting), requiring medical and lifestyle approaches for relief.

💊 Common Pain Relief Medicines

  • Paracetamol: For mild to moderate pain and fever
  • Ibuprofen: Reduces inflammation and pain
  • Diclofenac: Used for joint, muscle, and back pain
  • Tramadol: For moderate to severe pain (prescription)
  • Amitriptyline: Often used for nerve pain

Always follow dosage instructions and consult a doctor before use.

🌿 Natural & Non-Medicinal Methods

  • 🧘 Yoga & meditation for stress-related pain
  • 💆 Massage therapy for muscle tension
  • 🔥 Hot/cold compress for swelling or stiffness
  • 🏃 Gentle physical activity to reduce chronic pain
  • 🌿 Herbal remedies like turmeric or ginger (with caution)
