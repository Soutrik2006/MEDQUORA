Understanding Stomach Health

Stomach health plays a vital role in digestion and nutrient absorption. Poor stomach care can lead to issues like indigestion, ulcers, or acidity. A healthy gut supports overall well-being.

Foods That Support Digestion

  • Yogurt: Rich in probiotics to balance gut bacteria
  • Bananas: Soothing and rich in fiber
  • Ginger: Reduces bloating and nausea
  • Leafy Greens: Help with bowel movement
  • Oats: Support digestion with soluble fiber

Stomach Care Tips

  • Drink enough water daily
  • Chew food thoroughly
  • Eat at regular intervals
  • Limit processed and fried food
  • Exercise to stimulate digestion
  • Avoid excessive caffeine and alcohol
