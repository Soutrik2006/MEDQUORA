Cardiac Care Guidelines

Your heart, your health — handled with care.

🫀 What is Cardiac Care?

Cardiac care focuses on maintaining the health of the heart and blood vessels. It includes lifestyle changes, medication management, and regular monitoring of conditions such as hypertension, arrhythmia, and coronary artery disease.

💊 Recommended Medicines

  • Aspirin: Prevents blood clots
  • Atorvastatin: Lowers cholesterol
  • Metoprolol: Controls blood pressure & heart rate
  • Clopidogrel: Helps prevent heart attacks
  • Ramipril: ACE inhibitor for blood pressure and heart failure

Note: Take medicines only under a doctor's prescription.

🏃 Exercise & Lifestyle Suggestions

  • 🚶‍♂️ Daily 30-minute brisk walk
  • 🧘‍♀️ Yoga or meditation to manage stress
  • 🥗 Heart-healthy diet (low salt, low fat, more fruits/veggies)
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking & limit alcohol
  • 😴 Ensure 7–8 hours of quality sleep
