Our Commitment to Security

Your personal and health data is safe with us.

1. Data Encryption

All personal and health data is encrypted using 256-bit SSL encryption during transmission and at rest on our servers.

2. Secure Login & Authentication

We use secure authentication practices such as OTPs, password hashing, and session timeouts to ensure only you can access your account.

3. Data Access Control

Only authorized personnel have access to your information. All data access is logged and monitored for unusual activity.

4. Third-Party Compliance

Our partnered labs, doctors, and service providers comply with HIPAA and local privacy regulations to ensure continued data safety.

5. Report a Security Issue

If you believe you’ve found a vulnerability in our system, please contact our security team at security@medquora.com.
