Oral Care Guidelines

Bright smile, healthy life — Take charge of your oral hygiene.

🦷 Understanding Oral Health

Oral health is vital to overall well-being. Poor dental hygiene can lead to cavities, gum disease, and even heart issues. Brushing, flossing, and regular dental visits are the foundation of oral care.

🪥 Daily Oral Hygiene Routine

  • 🪥 Brush your teeth twice daily with fluoride toothpaste
  • 🧵 Floss once a day to remove plaque between teeth
  • 🧴 Use mouthwash to kill bacteria and freshen breath
  • 👅 Clean your tongue gently to prevent bad breath
  • 🚱 Avoid sugary foods and drinks between meals

Maintain consistency for long-term benefits.

🍎 Foods That Support Dental Health

  • Apples & Carrots: Naturally clean teeth and gums
  • Cheese & Yogurt: Strengthen enamel with calcium
  • Leafy Greens: Provide vitamins for gum health
  • Water: Keeps your mouth hydrated and clean

🩺 Dental Health Tips

  • 🦷 Visit the dentist every 6 months for checkups
  • 🚭 Quit tobacco and limit alcohol
  • 🛏️ Don’t skip nighttime brushing
  • 👶 Start oral care early for kids
  • 🛡️ Wear a mouthguard during sports
