Shipping & Delivery Policy

Everything you need to know about how we deliver your health essentials.

1. Shipping Coverage

We ship to most PIN codes across India. Check availability by entering your PIN code during checkout.

2. Delivery Timelines

  • Metros: 1–3 business days
  • Tier-2 & Tier-3 cities: 3–5 business days
  • Rural areas: Up to 7 business days

3. Shipping Charges

We offer free shipping on orders above ₹499. For smaller orders, a nominal fee of ₹49 is applied.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive an SMS/email with the tracking number. You can also track your order on the “My Orders” page.

5. Delivery Partners

We work with leading courier companies like Delhivery, Blue Dart, and India Post to ensure timely and safe deliveries.

6. In Case of Delays

While we strive to deliver on time, delays due to unforeseen events may occur. We’ll notify you via email/SMS if this happens.
