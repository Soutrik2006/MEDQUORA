Return Policy

Know your rights and options for returning products.

1. Eligibility for Return

You can return a product within 7 days of delivery if:

  • The item is damaged or defective
  • You received the wrong product
  • The item is expired or not as described

2. Items Not Eligible

  • Opened medicines or health supplements
  • Items without original packaging
  • Products marked as non-returnable

3. How to Request a Return

  1. Go to My Orders in your account
  2. Select the product you want to return
  3. Click on Request Return and follow the steps

4. Refund Process

After pickup and verification, your refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 working days.

5. Contact Support

If you face issues, contact our support team at support@medquora.com or call +91-1234567890.
